![Headshot from SuperComputing '22 in Dallas, Tx](headshot.jpg){: .image-right } Well, I guess I should tell you a little about me. Hello, I'm Caleb Fultz and I am a Tier III Linux System Admistrator for the National Radio Astronomy Observtory and the North American ALMA Science Center located in Charlottesville, VA. I have a over 15 years of working experience with Linux, over 25 with Windows, and almost my entire life with electronics and computers of all sizes. I'm a strong proponent of privacy, security, and freedom online and off. 

I'm a sponsor of [The Tor Project](https://www.torproject.org/) both monetarily and as a 5x Relay Operator. I created and moderate [/r/TomatoFTW](https://reddit.com/r/tomatoftw) and [/r/PlexPosters](https://reddit.com/r/plexposters).
